Biography

Yahaya Shehu Ahmed was born on 15th March 1975. He is married to Aishatu Abdullahi and Adama Muhammad Wadata, with whom he has ten children. He attended Zarumai Model School for his primary education and Government Day Secondary School for his secondary education. He obtained an NCE certificate and has since worked as a civil servant. His dedication to both family and career is widely respected, and he remains active in his professional and community engagements. You can reach him at 08062822368.
