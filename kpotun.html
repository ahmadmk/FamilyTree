Biography

Engr. Aliyu Shehu Ahmed is married to Halima Gogo Aliyu, a union that took place in January 2000. He began his early education at Ijaro Municipal School, Lagos, and continued at Capital School Minna before completing his primary studies at Zarumai Primary School Minna. He attended Barewa College, Zaria, and later Government Science College, Kagara for his secondary education. He proceeded to the Federal University of Technology, Minna, where he earned a Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) in Mechanical Engineering. He can be reached via 08056778201 or 08037876587.
