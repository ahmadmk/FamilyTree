Hajiya Fatima (Naku)

Hajiya Fatima, affectionately known as Naku, was one of the esteemed wives of Alhaji Shehu Katcha. She was blessed with eight children, contributing significantly to the Katcha family legacy. Her nurturing spirit and dedication to her family have left an indelible mark on all who knew her.
