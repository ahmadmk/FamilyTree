Biography

Mohammed Shehu Ahmed (Bakudu Baba Aji) was born on October 26, 1973. He attended Chiroma Primary School, Zarmai Primary School, and Ndayako Primary School for his early education. He proceeded to Government Science College, Kutigi, for his secondary education.He later gained admission into the Federal University of Technology, Minna, where he graduated in the year 2000.Mohammed Shehu Ahmed has built a career with the Nigerian Ports Authority, where he continues to contribute his expertise and experience.He is married to Fatima Larai Mohammed, and their union, which began in 2007, has been blessed with love and companionship.
