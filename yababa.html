Biography

Umar Shehu Ahmed, born on 30 April 1968, is married to three wives: Hauwa Umar Shehu, whom he married in February 1991; Maimuna Adamu, married on 1 January 2011; and Hauwa Muhammed, married on 28 March 2017. He began his education at Zarumai Primary School, then attended Bida Science Teacher College for his secondary education. He later studied at the National Open University, graduating with a Second-Class degree. He works with the National Identity Management Commission (NIMC).
