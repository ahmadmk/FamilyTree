Biography

Amina Shehu Ahmed (Talatu), born on 26 January 1971, is married to Isah Muhammad Makpa, an Assistant Comptroller with the Nigeria Immigration Service. They married on 5 March 1992. She attended Gaskiya Primary School, Tudun Jikum, Zaria, and later continued her secondary education at Government Day Secondary School, Zaria, and WATC Dikko. She furthered her studies at the School of Health, Kaduna, as well as the College of Education, Kaduna, where she graduated with qualifications in Social Studies/Arabic and Medical Laboratory. She worked professionally as a teacher. Her contact number is 08030710388.
