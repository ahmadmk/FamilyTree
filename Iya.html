Hajiya Iya

Hajiya Iya, the wife of the late Alhaji Ahmed Shehu Katcha, is a respected matriarch within the Katcha family. She is the proud mother of nine children, each of whom has contributed to the family's enduring legacy. Her unwavering support and dedication to her family have been a cornerstone of their strength and unity.
