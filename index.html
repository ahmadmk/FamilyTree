Biography

Alhaji Shehu Katcha (1930-2007) was a distinguished Nigerian public servant. He served as Secretary to the State Government (SMG) and Head of Service (HOS) from 1981 to 1983. Prior to this, he worked with the Nigerian Railway Corporation (NRC), eventually becoming General Manager. Following the creation of Niger State in 1976, Alhaji Katcha held various ministerial positions as a Permanent Secretary, before rising to SMG/Head of Service, a position he retired from in 1983.Notably, under the leadership of General Ibrahim Babangida, he was appointed Chairman of the Code of Conduct Bureau in Niger State from 1989 to 1999. Alhaji Shehu Katcha passed away on August 14, 2007, leaving behind a lasting legacy in public service.
