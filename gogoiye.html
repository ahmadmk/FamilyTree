Zainab S Ahmed (Gogoiye)

Biography

She is married to Liman Alhassan Mohammed, and was born on 16th May 1977. She got married on 5th March 1992. She began her early education at Bangiful Model Primary School, Katcha, where she studied from 1980 to 1986. She continued to Government Girls Secondary School, Katcha, between 1987 and 1990. In her commitment to furthering her Islamic education, she attended Madarasatul Nur’Faidah wa Tahfeez Li’Quran Islamic School, Wunangi, under Mallam Muhammad Jibril Alfa, from 2007 to 2011, where she obtained a Certificate in Qur’an Memorization. She is currently an entrepreneur, dedicated to her work and her family.
