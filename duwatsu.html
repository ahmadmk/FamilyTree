Biography

Mohammed Duwastu Ahmadu was born on 13th January 1966 and is married to Hadiza Sanda Ndayako, a union established on 28th January 2000. He began his education at Ijero Municipal Grammar School, Lagos, and later attended Zarumai Primary School. For secondary school, he studied at Barewa College, Zaria, and Government Secondary School, Suleja. He went on to obtain a Diploma in Personnel Management from Kaduna Polytechnic, followed by an HND in Marketing from Federal Polytechnic Birnin Kebbi, and later earned a Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria. He further achieved an MBA (Master of Business Administration). Professionally, he served as a civil servant and also worked in the banking sector, and is now retired.
